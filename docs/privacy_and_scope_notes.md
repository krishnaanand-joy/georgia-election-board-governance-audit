# Privacy and Scope Notes

This repository is a sanitized public case study.

## What is included

This repo includes:

- A public case-study README
- A mock data schema
- Fictional sample rows
- Methodology notes
- Outreach workflow notes
- A small script for summarizing mock records

## What is not included

This repo does not include:

- The original ACLU master spreadsheet
- Internal ACLU work product
- County-specific findings
- Legal conclusions about specific counties
- Raw call notes
- Raw email text
- Names, emails, or phone numbers of county officials
- Any private or strategic information

## Why the original data is excluded

The internal project involved legal and policy research, direct outreach, and review-sensitive notes. Publishing raw records could misrepresent unresolved findings, expose contact details, or disclose internal work product.

The public version is designed to show the workflow and research approach without exposing sensitive information.

## Scope limitation

This repository does not make claims about whether any specific Georgia county is legally compliant or noncompliant.

Any real-world governance or compliance determinations should be made by qualified legal or policy staff using original sources and proper review procedures.
